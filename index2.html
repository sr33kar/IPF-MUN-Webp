DISEC

AGENDA: Combating the proliferation and trade of small arms with special emphasis on the Arms Trade Treaty.

The Disarmament and International Security (DISEC) constitutes the ‘First Committee’ of the General Assembly of the United Nations.

The First Committee (DISEC) is responsible for tackling issues related to disarmament and threats to global peace. Disarmament is the reduction or withdrawal of military forces and weapons and DISEC deals in matters of regulation of armaments, promotion of cooperative arrangements and measures to strengthen global security.
