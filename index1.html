OVERVIEW

NITC MUN is a 2-day conference organized by the Industrial Planning Forum (IPF), NIT Calicut. At NITC MUN, delegates gain insight into the functioning of the United Nations, develop cultural empathy and expand their knowledge of international affairs by assuming the role of international decision makers. The NITC MUN Conference conducted by IPF has consolidated itself as being one of the signature MUN's in Kerala and South India.

NITC MUN is an exciting platform for the young leaders of tomorrow to debate the most pressing issues of today. It equips the delegates with analytical, reasoning and leadership skills and in the process enables them to arrive at creative and innovative solutions.
