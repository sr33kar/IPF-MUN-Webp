UNHRC

AGENDA: Protecting rights of de jure stateless persons with special reference to repatriation.

The United Nations Human Rights Council(UNHRC) is a United Nations body that deals with the promotion and protection of human rights around the globe.

It commits to addressing situations involving human rights violations and finding plausible solutions for the same.
The UNHRC investigates allegations of breaches of human rights in UN member states, and addresses important thematic human rights issues such as freedom of association and assembly,freedom of expression,freedom of belief and religion,women's rights,LGBT rights,and the rights of racial and ethnic minorities.
